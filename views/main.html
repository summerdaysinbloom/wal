Features

Intuitive Electronic Platform

With our intuitive platform, making payments have never been easier or more efficient. With all your payments under one roof, you can analyze your transaction and conversion history to streamline your business into a lean mean machine.

Multi-currency Management

Wallex allow users to hold balances in multiple currencies, enabling them to convert freely from one currency to another whenever needed. This allows them to manage their FX exposure efficiently and to also be nimble enough to take advantage of volatility in the currency markets.

Revolving line of credit

We finance 5 to 8 times the balance in your Wallex account up to 1M USD for your payment needs in any currencies.

How it works

Tell us where to send the money

Provide us with your invoice and tell us where to send the money. We only take the details we need to make a successful payment.

Convert

Lock in the conversion rate whenever you are ready. We provide you with the best rates available in the market and will hold it for you for 4 hours.

Fund your payment

Send money to our local bank accounts to fund your transaction. Your payment will be processed and released upon receipt of funds.
